Social Circle
2013

This work represents the evolution of a social circle of shapes, where the influence of the larger group compels a minority to resemble them in order to stay in the circle. Move your mouse over the piece to go further in time and see the evolution of the social circle. Based on "Untitled" by Edward Zajec, originally published in "Computer Graphics and Art" v2n4, 1977. Part of the ReCode Project. Created with Processing / p5.js.
