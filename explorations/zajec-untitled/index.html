Untitled (after Edward Zajec)
2013

Direct translation of Edward Zajec's "Untitled" from 1977. Each figure is drawn by a separate function, with values carefully studied to be as accurate as possible to the original piece. Originally published in "Computer Graphics and Art" v2n4, 1977. Part of the ReCode Project - a community-driven effort to preserve and recreate computer art from the past. Created with Processing / p5.js.
